An outstanding sequal to an outstanding game.  Red Alert was Warcraft 2's competition, and the sequal is a great game.  With the expansion pack, Yuri's Revenge, You get to be a completly original force.  The Yuri troops have all mind control ability, which adds quite an odd factor to the gameplay.

Yuri's Revenge won PCGamer's Expansion Pack of the Year Award for 2001.
